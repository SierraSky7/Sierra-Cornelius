about me

A dynamic, creative, and resourceful media artist experienced in concept creation, storytelling, graphic design, writing, and strategic communications. Passionate about the vital role that visual art and writing play in transmitting valuable ideas that inform and inspire, she hopes to advance her 3+ years of professional design experience in the creative industry.

my work


art


My dream when I was a child was to grow up and become an artist. I have always loved to paing, draw, doodle, and generally create. I wholeheartedly believe that art was instrumental in managing my ADHD symptoms before I was diagnosed and my ability to hyperfocus on a project has allowed me to be a better artist and accomplish more than others who might become otherwise frustrated. I've also always had a rebellious streak when it comes to art and I prefer to break the rules rather than adhere to them. Creativity should not be caged. My creative passion is something that I am honored to share with my children and their tactile imaginations are far superior to what mine could ever be. literature

read all of the books

My love and passion for reading began very early on in my life. Stories were a way for me to escape, travel, explore, learn, and meet new people. I fell in love with that moment when you are reading about an experience either fictional or not of someone whose life looks entirely unline yours and your soul expands just a little more to make room for theirs. Books and characters have been my favorite relationships and friends. I believe that stories are a sacred and powerful tool for change. Explore Maria Popova's blog that has and endless supply of thought-provoking explorations on the power of story and "what it means to live a decent, substantive, rewarding life."
